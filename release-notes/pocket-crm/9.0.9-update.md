# Pocket CRM 9.0.9 update

## The app now supports alarms for iOS 13 devices

Pocket CRM has been updated for iOS 13.

![Mobile -app-screen][img1]

This means that alarms are now supported for users who have upgraded their mobile devices to iOS 13 as well.
